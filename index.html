Update: Survivor accounts and deportee figures confirmed; ICE non-response reported
146 US deportees were taken to a La Guaira hotel hours before the earthquakes struck; many are missing. A deportation flight from Miami landed in Venezuela hours before Wednesday's twin earthquakes, carrying 146 Venezuelans including 19 women and seven children, according to ICE Flight Monitor. They were transported to Hotel Santuario La Llanada in La Guaira, one of the hardest-hit areas. Survivors described escaping rubble; others remain missing. ICE did not respond to an AP request for comment. →
