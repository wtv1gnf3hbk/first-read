Supreme Court strikes down Trump birthright citizenship order, 6-3. The Supreme Court voted 6-3 to uphold birthright citizenship, rejecting Trump's executive order that would have denied citizenship to children born to parents in the U.S. illegally or temporarily. Chief Justice Roberts, joined by Barrett and the three liberals, held the 14th Amendment confers citizenship at birth. Justice Kavanaugh concurred on statutory grounds. Justices Alito, Gorsuch, and Thomas dissented. Trump called the ruling 'too bad for our Country' and urged Congress to act legislatively. →
